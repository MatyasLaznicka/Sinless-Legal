Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How does the subscription work?

A: Sinless requires a subscription to access all features. Your subscription includes unlimited daily tasks, AI-powered guidance from Wise, personalized Bible verses, and tier progression tracking. Subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID.

Q: How do I cancel my subscription?

A: To cancel your subscription, go to Settings > Your Name > Subscriptions on your iPhone, find Sinless, and tap "Cancel Subscription." You'll retain access until the end of your current billing period.

Q: Can I restore my subscription on a new device?

A: Yes! Sign in with the same Apple ID you used for your original purchase, and your subscription will automatically restore.

Q: How does the tier system work?

A: Complete daily tasks to earn points and progress through 4 tiers: Seeker (0-249 pts), Warrior (250-749 pts), Guardian (750-1,549 pts), and Conquerer (1,550+ pts). Each completed task earns you 5 points.

Q: What happens if I miss a day?

A: Your progress and points are saved, but your daily streak will reset. New tasks are generated each day, so you can always continue your journey.

Q: Is my data private and secure?

A: Yes. Your personal data is encrypted and securely stored. We never share your information with third parties. See our Privacy Policy section below for more details.

Q: Can I change my sin profile?

A: Currently, your sin profile is set during onboarding. If you'd like to retake the assessment, please contact support.
